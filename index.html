Paquetes

Let us help make your special event a memory that will last a lifetime!

¡Déjanos ayudarte a que tu evento especial sea un recuerdo que perdure toda la vida!

Corporate – Parties – Meetings – Seminars – Weddings, Sweet Sixteen, Quinceañeras, and Bachelor Parties, Baptisms, Funerals, Anniversaries, or any occasion for celebrating. We offer packages ranging from basic to complete, or we can tailor a package to your party's needs. All packages include tables, chairs, basic decorations, cake table, main table, gift table, 2 waitresses, bartender, 2 security personnel, unlimited ice and soda. Please contact us for pricing details.

Corporativos – Fiestas – Reuniones – Seminarios – Bodas, Dulces Dieciséis, Quinceañeras y Despedidas de Soltero, Bautizos, Funerales, Aniversarios, o cualquier ocasión para celebrar. Ofrecemos paquetes que van desde básicos hasta completos, o podemos adaptar un paquete a las necesidades de tu fiesta. Todos los paquetes incluyen mesas, sillas, decoraciones básicas, mesa de pastel, mesa principal, mesa de regalos, 2 meseras, bartender, 2 personas de seguridad, hielo y refrescos ilimitados. Por favor, contáctanos para detalles de precios.

Rental Package for 300 people

  • • Round Tables, Long Tables, and Chairs
  • • Servers – from 5pm to 9pm – No time change
  • • Bartender for 300 people – 6pm to 1am
  • • Unlimited soda and ice – 300 people only additional charges over 300 people.
  • • Event Hall

Additional Packages:

  • • Offered: Table cloths, white + any other color, seat covers plus matching bows – seat covers + bows. long table cloths
  • • An additional 1 hour of music, past 1 am, is provided at the discretion of management for an additional charge. The charges are to be paid in advance and arranged at the time of payment for hall rental. The cost will be determined by management.
  • • Cleaning of the event hall is offered for an additional charge, and must be arranged at the time of booking the hall.

Paquete de Alquiler para 300 personas

  • • Mesas Redondas, Mesas Largas y Sillas
  • • Meseros – de 5pm a 9pm – Sin cambio de horario
  • • Barman para 300 personas – de 6pm a 1am
  • • Refrescos y hielo ilimitados – solo para 300 personas, cargos adicionales por más de 300 personas.
  • • Salón de Eventos

Paquetes Adicionales:
